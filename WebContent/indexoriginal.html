About Us

Here at Master Paint Services we know that taking care of your house requires much atention and energy, usually attempting to fix an issue reveals a handful of other problems, that's why we are ready to solve everything that stands between you and the house of your dreams.

Maintenance

This is one of the first things to consider in order to keep a beautiful house, it helps preserving all areas wether interior or exterior of your house. Maintenance is an useful procedure to find out what we need to do to prevent future accidents and issues.

Repairing

Our objective is to evaluate the posibilities to work and return the damaged areas to their normal condition or improving it in general.

Painting

Our service has no limitations when it comes to paint. If you want a simple retouch we'll be there. We can do a variety of services both in exteriors and interiors based on your own criteria. We'll adjust to your needs and time constraints. And we'll make sure that all the areas we work are left clean and orderly.

We are 100% ensured and licensed!

Services

These are our specialities

  • Interior & Exterior:

    Complete renovation for the whole house

    Our services guarantee every nick and cranny will be covered. We are ready to repair a wide variety of elements to ensure our clients' convenience.

  • Power Washing:

    Remove the hardest stains in seconds

    As time goes by, great amounts of sediments gather on all surfaces of your house, especially on the floor, making it look dirty and old no matter how much you scrub it with an average brush. With our service you can say goodbye to the dirt in the blink of an eye. This also indicates when it's necessary to paint your house.

  • Drywall Repair:

    Have your walls look like new agains

    You don't have to worry anymore about covering those bumbs and holes in the walls with sofas and pictures. With our repairing services those walls will look like perfect.

  • Popcorn Ceiling Removal:

    Improve the esthetics of your rooms

    The old standart of popcorn ceiling has long been abandoned due to the poor looks it gives to rooms and so it has been replaced with new modern styles. It's also recommended to remove it because of the materials used in it like asbestos, known to be a harmful carcinogenic.

  • Stain and Varnish:

    Restore the woods in your doors and garages

    Wooden doors, fences, handholds and garages give a spectacular look to your house, but the sunlight and the rain can wear it's colors out slowly if left unchecked. Our services will leave all your wooden surfaces like brand new.

  • Floor covering and Wood Rot Repairs:

    Vanish all traces of humidity from your house

    One of the most common problems in homes and residences are leakings, they can make terrible damages to your house destroying wooden floors. With our services you can free your home from these problems and see your floors shinny as they should be.

  • Roof Repair, Residential & Commercial:

    Take care of both your house and your business

    If you have a commercial building that needs to be checked out, you can count on us as well. Our services will ease your mind from the little imperfections in your roofs like cracks, leaks and much more.
